Installation Instructions:
  1. Tap "Download App" above
  2. Your browser will download a .mobileconfig file
  3. Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management
  4. Under "Downloaded Profile", tap the movies/series profile
  5. Tap "Install" in the top right
  6. Enter your passcode if prompted
  7. Tap "Install" again to confirm
  8. The app icon will appear on your home screen
